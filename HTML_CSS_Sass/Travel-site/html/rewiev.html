5 Tips For Writing Travel Reviews

As a traveler, you have basically two expectations placed on you: enjoy the heck out of your trip, and share the story of your trip with others. The first one is easy – after all, that’s why you travel, right? The second one can be a bit challenging, but is so important, especially in today’s hyper-connected social culture. Odds are, when you were shopping for your flight, hotel, or things to do, you read reviews along the way. As such, when you get back from your trip, it’s a good idea to spend a bit of time posting a review of your own, to pay it forward and help future travelers know what to expect, questions to ask, or things to avoid. This can be daunting if you’re not a writer by nature. Don’t worry – we’re here to help! Below are five tips to get you started.
